In the beginning . . .

A neighborhood favorite and Louisville staple for over 20 years, Buckhead Mountain Grill is a locally owned, family-friendly restaurant. We combine a comfortable and relaxed mountain lodge atmosphere with a full selection of classic, delectable foods. From our famous Cheese Boulders to delicious, homemade Pot Pie, guests have been enjoying many of our signature dishes for years.

Our large menu offers something for everyone, including meat loaf, pot pies, steak and ribs, as well as lighter fare for warm-weather dining. Made from original recipes, our delicious choices are always fresh and made by hand every day. These are real meals made in a real kitchen! And, of course, let’s not forget about our wide variety of beers. At Buckhead, we’re dedicated to bringing you a great selection of craft beers from around the world. So come on in and have a pint with us. You won’t be disappointed.

Visit one of three locations, either in the greater Louisville area or in Bellevue, Kentucky – just minutes from downtown Cincinnati.

Location, location, location. We have three.

Charity Days

Buckhead Mountain Grill is passionate about giving back to the communities that we serve. Our Charity Days fundraising program helps schools, non-profits, and sports groups raise money by donating a portion of Buckhead sales back to your organization. Since the beginning of this charitable effort, we’ve donated over $40,000 back into the community and want to continue this effort by getting your organization or school involved!

Are you a school, school group, or 501c3 organization looking for proceeds? Send us an email to get your Charity Day scheduled and we’ll donate 20% of sales from that day back to your organization.
